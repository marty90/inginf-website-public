Alberto Bartoli

Jun 15, 2022
1 min read

Alberto Bartoli received the degree (cum laude) in electrical engineering and the Ph.D. degree in computer engineering from the University of Pisa, Pisa, Italy, in 1989 and 1993, respectively.,Since 1998, he has been an Associate Professor with the Department of Engineering and Architecture, University of Trieste, Trieste, Italy, where he is the Director of Machine Learning Lab. His research interests include machine learning applications, evolutionary computation, and security.,Dr. Bartoli received the Silver Medal at the 13th Annual “Humies” Awards for Human-Competitive Results Produced by Genetic and Evolutionary Computation in 2016.
